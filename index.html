Hi, I’m Hemanth, a passionate **AWS DevOps Engineer** with **2.6 years** of hands-on experience in building and managing scalable cloud infrastructure and automating the continuous delivery pipeline. I thrive on implementing strategies like **blue-green and canary deployments** to ensure seamless feature rollouts and collaborate effectively with development teams using advanced Git workflows.
